# Power-BI-Business-Sales-Performance

## Project Overview

![Dashboard](https://github.com/Hatem207/Power-BI-Business-Sales-Performance/blob/main/Business%20Sales%20Dashboard.png)

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of business sales performance. It enables decision-makers to monitor revenue trends, evaluate product performance, analyze sales distribution, and gain valuable business insights through interactive dashboards and key performance indicators (KPIs).

## Business Problem

Businesses require an interactive reporting solution to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing products, evaluate sales trends, and support data-driven business decisions for improving revenue and operational efficiency.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning
* Data Analysis
* Data Visualization

## Key KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Average Sales
* Sales Growth
* Top Performing Products
* Sales by Category
* Sales Distribution

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Overview

* Monitor overall business sales performance.
* Analyze sales trends over time.
* Compare sales across different product categories.

### Product Analysis

* Identify top-selling products.
* Compare product performance by sales value.
* Evaluate product contribution to total revenue.

### Category Analysis

* Compare sales across business categories.
* Identify high-performing and low-performing categories.
* Analyze category contribution to overall sales.

### Trend Analysis

* Track sales performance over time.
* Identify growth and seasonal patterns.
* Monitor changes in business performance.

### Interactive Filters

* Product Filtering
* Category Filtering
* Date Filtering
* Dynamic Power BI Visualizations

## Key Insights

* Sales performance varies significantly across product categories.
* A small number of products contribute a large percentage of total sales.
* Interactive filtering helps identify business opportunities quickly.
* Sales trends provide valuable insights for forecasting and planning.
* The dashboard supports fast and data-driven business decisions.
